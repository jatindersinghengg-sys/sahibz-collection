Sahib'z Collection is a ladies boutique in Phagwara, Punjab, run by Navkiran Kaur. We handpick premium quality suits — embroidered cotton, printed cotton, Gadhval silk, and Warli cotton — for women who love to dress beautifully.

We believe every woman deserves beautiful clothes without having to travel far. That's why we deliver across India, right to your door, at honest prices.
